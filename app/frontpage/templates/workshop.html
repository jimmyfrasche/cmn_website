Workshop on Open and Reproducible Neuroscience

NIMH's Data Science and Sharing Team will be conducting a workshop on open and reproducible neuroscience on Aug 1st and 2nd. This hands on workshop will cover:

  • Using Python, NiPype, and github to produce & share reproducible processing pipelines (Satra Ghosh)
  • Using git-annex and DataLad to share and re-use data (Yarik Halchenko)
  • Using containers on the NIH HPC system (Wolfgang Resch)

Tues, Aug 1 & Wed, Aug 2, 9 am – 5 pm
NIH Cloisters, Rathskeller (Bldg 60, Room 036)

Note that space in the course is limited. If you'd like to attend, please sign up for the workshop here.

Course Leads and Guests Speakers

  • Satra Ghosh, MIT
  • Yarik Halchenko, Dartmouth
  • Wolfgang Resch, NIH HPC Team
  • Adam Thomas
  • John Lee
  • Dylan Nielson
